do you charge per-photo or per-product?
We charge per-photo. This means that if you have 2 products and want 4 photos each, you will be charged for 8 photos.
how much will it cost?
The real answer, it depends. The biggest factor is how many images you decide to order at the end. Please contact us to have a final cost estimation.
how do I pay?
We currently only accept PayPal or cash if we meet in Cupertino.
what if I have a product that is overly large in size?
Normally, we don't shoot overly large sized products and only stick with small to medium sized products. Let us know in advanced if we can accommodate to your product. If we can, there is an extra $10 per image fee.
if I live in the Bay Area, can I bring it to you?
Yes, of course! We encourage that! We can meet at a coffee shop to drop off your product and we can chat in-person about how you specifically want things done.
how long do photography projects take?
From the time we receive your product, our photoshoot/editing process takes about 5-7 business days. If you have a large number of products or photos requests, this may add to the production time. Please contact us if you want a confirmed date.
what comes with each image?
Each image comes with a solid white/black background (other colors are available upon request). We use coloring grading and enhancement to ensure we are accurately protraying your product in an amazing way. Also, scratches, smudges, and other minor defeats will be removed via Photoshop. Visit our 'pricing' tab to check out what's included and upgrade possibilities.
do you take lifestyle pictures?
Yes, we take basic lifestyle pictures with minor props. Contact us to make sure we can accommodate to the natural settings of your product. The cost of props purchased for the lifestyle pictures are added to the final invoice.
any services that you don't offer?
Yes, we don't offer jewelry, clothing, and glassware photography and modeling with your products.
what if I want an image to be taken again?
If we've made a mistake, we will gladly retake or edit the pictures to make them right for no charge. However, if it's from client communication error, the reshoot charges will be added to the end invoice.
in what format are my photos delivered?
Standard product photos are delivered as a high-resolution JPG and a transparent PNG. For an extra $5 per image, you can have the original Photoshop file for future editing.
how do you deliver my finished photos?
We will provide you a link to download your images off of our website. You have 60 days to retrieve it off our websites and then will be taken off our database for storage reasons. An extra $5 charge would be added if needed to access these photos past the 60 day period.
do you ship my products back when you're done?
Of course! We include the cost of return shipping in your final invoice or we can meet in person to return your product. Another option is for us to donate your product to a local charity or Salvation Army.
do I have to pay royalty for each time I use it?
Nope! After purchasing your images, you are free to use it as many times as you want, for as long as you want. We allow the use of these photos in all forms of media including print advertising, packaging, web, television, etc.
