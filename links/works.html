1. contact me
Let me know what your product is, the size of your product(s), how many products you have, and the total number of images you are interested in purchasing.
2. create a shot list
I need to know your style and what you like! Please send me some example pictures either found on the Internet, or shot (with a cell phone, it doesn't matter) by you that would serve as a guideline for us. Use this excel spreadsheet as a guide and send it to me.
3. send in your product or meet me
Send me your product or save on shipping costs and meet me in the Cupertino area! I'm a Bay Area local and I would love to discuss your wants/desires in-person over some coffee with you.
4. the photograph & editing process
Your products will be taken using professional camera gear and lighting equipment and edited with Adobe Photoshop. My standard turn around time is 5-7 business days from the time I receive the product. My Rush Time, for an extra $10 per photo, would be 3 business days from the time I receive the product.
5. review and approve images
Pay for only what you want to pay for! I'll give you a link to our pictures and let us know if you want any changes. Use the "download" link to fully download the high-resolution photos. All images come with unlimited minor Photoshop adjustments. Watermarks will be visible until payment is received.
6. payment & return of your product
If you want to return your product, let me know. Price of return shipping is included in your final invoice or I can meet in person to return your product. Another option is for me to donate your product to a local organization.
